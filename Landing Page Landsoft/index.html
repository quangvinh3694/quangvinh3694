GetResponse Landing Pages explained

What is a landing page?

A landing page is a tool that you can use to convert your visitors into customers. Typically a landing page consists of key content, offers, and an easy point of sale for prospective clients to register for or purchase your goods and services.

How can I use and benefit from landing pages?

By crafting well-designed landing pages, you can start turning your site visitors into paying customers. Landing pages can also be used as sign-up pages to further build your email list. They can be utilized through interlinking within your website or other external means such as email blast links or organic search results.

What are landing page templates?

Let’s face it. Not all of us are graphic designers – and that’s ok. Landing page templates are predesigned layouts that you can customize without needing any design experience. Simply adjust ready-made designs to fit your particular niche and business needs.

What’s the difference between landing page and website?

A landing page is just that. It’s a place where you can send a prospective client in order to convert. They are often used as part of a website as a whole. A website features much more than just landing pages including blog content, about and contact pages, and other online resources.
